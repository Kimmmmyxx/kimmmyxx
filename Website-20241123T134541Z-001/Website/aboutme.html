ABOUT
ME

My name is Kimberly Perez, and I’m 19 years old. I’ve always had a passion for drawing, especially portraits and random creative pieces. I initially pursued a Bachelor of Multimedia Arts (BMMA), but I eventually decided to shift to Information Technology (IT). While I thought I could handle the challenges of the BMMA program, I quickly realized it wasn’t as easy as I expected. It was mentally and emotionally draining, which led me to make the decision to transition to IT, where I feel I can thrive more. Alongside my studies, I also manage a coffee shop that my sister originally owned. She moved abroad, and I took on the responsibility of running the business in her absence. Before becoming the manager, I worked as a barista, which I absolutely loved. It was such a rewarding experience, and I had the opportunity to meet so many amazing people and make lifelong friendships.
